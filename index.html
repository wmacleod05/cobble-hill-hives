Cobble Hill Hives is a locally run honey compnay run by Mr. and Mrs. Macleod.

About Us

We are a local group in Brooklyn with Hives in our backyard since 2017. Mr. Morris Macleod handles and takes care of the bees along with harvesting and packaging the honey.

Products

For many years now, we have harvested and packaged pounds of honey waiting to be distributed. We harvest the honey in a safe and effective manner and package said honey in jars with our logo on it which we distribute to buyers or families and friends.
