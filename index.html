Welcome to Cobble Hill Hives

Cobble Hill Hives is a locally run honey company founded by Mr. and Mrs. Macleod in Brooklyn, New York. We've been keeping bees in our backyard since 2017 and harvest our honey with care and tradition.

Our Story

We started as a small neighborhood project and quickly realized how much joy pure, natural honey brings to people. Mr. Morris Macleod handles the bees, harvests the honey, and packages it by hand. Our honey is raw, local, and delicious.
